### Primary Colors
- **Background Gradient:** Purple (#667eea) to Deep Purple (#764ba2)
- **Cards:** White (#ffffff)

- **Success/Active:** Green (#4CAF50)
- **Warning/Queue:** Orange (#FF9800)
- **Info:** Blue (#2196F3)
- **Error:** Red (#f44336)
