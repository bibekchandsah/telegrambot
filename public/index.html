How It Works

Start chatting in just 3 simple steps

1

Open the Bot

Click the "Open in Telegram" button or search for @YourBotUsername on Telegram and press Start.

2

Use /chat Command

Type /chat and you'll be instantly matched with a random person from anywhere in the world.

3

Start Chatting!

Begin your anonymous conversation. Share messages, media, and connect with interesting people worldwide.

Available Commands

/start Start the bot and see welcome message
/chat Find a random chat partner
/stop End current conversation
/next Skip to next person
/preferences Set gender & country filters
/report Report inappropriate behavior
